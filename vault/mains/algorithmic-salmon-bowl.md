# Algorithmic Salmon Bowl

A fast, macro-balanced salmon bowl focused on Omega-3 delivery and minimal prep time.

## Ingredients
- 6oz Salmon fillet
- 1 cup cooked quinoa or brown rice
- 1/2 avocado, sliced
- 1/4 cup edamame
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp honey
- Sriracha to taste
- Furikake seasoning

## Process
1. **Marinade:** Whisk soy sauce, sesame oil, honey, and a dash of sriracha. Brush over the salmon.
2. **Execute:** Air fry the salmon at 400°F for 8-10 minutes until easily flaked with a fork.
3. **Assemble:** Place warm quinoa in a bowl. Top with air-fried salmon, sliced avocado, and edamame. 
4. **Garnish:** Drizzle any remaining marinade over the top and sprinkle generously with furikake.
