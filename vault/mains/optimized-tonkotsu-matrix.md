# Optimized Tonkotsu Matrix

A highly refined, macro-optimized take on traditional Tonkotsu ramen. 

## Ingredients
- 2 lbs pork femur bones
- 1 lb chicken trotters
- 1 onion, halved
- 1 head of garlic, halved horizontally
- 2 portions fresh ramen noodles
- 80g Chashu pork (lean cut)
- 2 soft boiled eggs (Ajitsuke Tamago)
- Tare (Soy sauce, mirin, sake, kelp)

## Process
1. **Bone Blanching:** Boil bones for 10 minutes to remove impurities. Discard water and scrub bones clean.
2. **The Matrix Boil:** Combine cleaned bones, onion, and garlic in a pressure cooker. Fill with water to the max line. Cook on high pressure for 3 hours, or simmer on stovetop for 8 hours until the broth is opaque and creamy.
3. **Assembly:** Add 30ml of Tare to a warm bowl. Strain the broth and pour over the Tare. 
4. **Execution:** Add cooked noodles. Top with Chashu, halved eggs, and a dash of black garlic oil.
