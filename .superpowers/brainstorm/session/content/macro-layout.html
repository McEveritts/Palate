Since we're using the "Curated Feed" approach for Macros, how should we organize the presentation?

A

Stat-Heavy Cards

Keep the exact same masonry layout as the Vault and Curated pages, but pre-sort them strictly by protein density, relying on the glowing macro badges we already built.
