The Digital Mushroom: 3 Iterations

Exploring the clash between the organic "Umami" shape and technology/AI.

1. The Circuit-Shroom

Hard geometric angles. The gills are rendered as negative-space data slots, and the stem is a literal circuit board.

2. Organic to Digital

A smooth, natural cap representing food/flavor, but the stem dissolves into raw pixels/data blocks.

3. The Neural Node

Leans heavily into the AI/Genie aspect. The mushroom shape is constructed entirely out of a glowing neural network diagram.
