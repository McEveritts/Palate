Smart Collections: Structural Approach

How should the Smart Collections (Macro-optimized, Zero-waste, Flavor-profile) behave?

A

The "Interactive Dashboard" (Recommended)

Each collection has a control panel at the top. For Macro-optimized, you set your target protein/calories and it dynamically sorts the vault. For Zero-waste, you select ingredients you want to use up.

B

The "Curated Feeds" (Simpler)

Collections act as pre-sorted static feeds. Macro-optimized simply highlights high-protein/low-calorie meals. Zero-waste highlights recipes with the highest ingredient overlap with your common pantry items.

C

The "Ask Sage" Integration

Collections are just specialized interfaces for Sage. Clicking "Zero-waste" opens a chat interface specifically tuned for pantry-clearing, rather than a traditional grid of recipes.
