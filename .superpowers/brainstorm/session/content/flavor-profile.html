Flavor-Profile: Structural Approach

We have Macros (Stat Cards) and Zero-Waste (Chat). How should the Flavor-Profile collection behave?

A

Mood & Cravings Swimlanes

Horizontal scrolling rows categorized by subjective culinary moods: "Comfort Food", "Light & Fresh", "Spicy & Bold", "Umami Bombs".

B

Interactive Flavor Map (Radar/Tags)

A visual UI (like a radar chart or tag cloud) where users select primary flavor notes (Sweet, Salty, Sour, Bitter, Umami, Spicy) to dynamically filter the vault.

C

The "Pairings" View

Focuses on cohesion. Select a main dish, and it presents mathematically and culinarily complementary sides and drinks to build a complete flavor profile.
