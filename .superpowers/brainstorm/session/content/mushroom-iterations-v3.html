Tweaking the Flavor Gradient

Focusing on the warm-to-cool aesthetic. Which specific execution feels best?

1. The Tech Overlay

Keeps the solid gradient cap and blocky stem, but overlays subtle circuit traces onto the "food" part of the logo.

2. Abstract Flow

Smoother, more stylized. The gradient flows seamlessly down, and the stem is made of vertical data streams rather than pixel blocks.

3. Neon Outline

Removes the solid fill entirely. The logo becomes a sleek, glowing neon wireframe that feels very at home in the AetherFlow aesthetic.
