Tweaking the Drip

Focusing solely on the geometric transition between the organic cap and the fading stem.

1. Seamless Curve

Removes the sharp pinch entirely. The cap flows smoothly inward like an hourglass before dropping the data lines.

2. Sharp V

Creates a sharper, more aggressive point. It looks less like a liquid drip and more like a precise funnel channeling data.

3. Stepped Tech

The fluid wave shape drops into rigid, stepped blocks, intentionally contrasting the smooth top with the digital bottom.
