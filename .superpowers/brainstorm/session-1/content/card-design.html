Card Design Focus

Since we're building a Masonry Grid, what should the cards prioritize at a glance?

A

Visuals & Vibe (AetherFlow)

Emphasis on abstract glassmorphic shapes, glowing category tags, and the recipe title. Macros and details only appear when you hover over the card.

B

Data-Rich (Nutrition First)

Calories, Protein, Carbs, and Fat are always visible in a sleek row at the bottom of the card, along with the title and tags. Best if you track macros closely.

C

Process Snippet

Shows the title, tags, and a tiny sneak peek of the ingredients list or the first step of the recipe right on the card.
