Phase 6: Vault Visualization Ideas

How should we visualize your local recipe vault?

A

Masonry Grid / Cards

A visual gallery of recipe cards featuring glowing tags, macro summaries, and glassmorphic hover states. Best for browsing.

B

High-Density List

A sleek, data-rich table or list view prioritizing macros, category, and quick access. Best for meal planning and efficiency.

C

Interactive Knowledge Graph

A node-based visualization linking recipes by shared ingredients and macro profiles. Highly experimental and visual.
