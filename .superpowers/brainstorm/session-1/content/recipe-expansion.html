Recipe Expansion Interaction

When you click a card to see the full recipe, how should it open?

A

Glassmorphic Modal

A beautiful glowing modal pops up in the center of the screen, blurring the grid behind it. Close it to return exactly where you left off.

B

Side Drawer / Panel

A sleek panel slides out from the right side of the screen. You can still see part of the grid on the left.

C

Expand In-Place

The card smoothly expands right where it is, pushing the surrounding masonry cards out of the way. Highly interactive and fluid.

D

Dedicated Page

Navigate to a completely separate, distraction-free page for that specific recipe (e.g., `/vault/mains/tzatziki-burger`).
