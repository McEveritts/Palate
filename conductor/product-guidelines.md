# Product Guidelines

## 1. Prose Style
**Refined & Precise**
All copy within the application, including the AI persona (Sage), must be elegant, concise, and professional. Avoid colloquialisms, robotic filler phrases, and excessive enthusiasm.

## 2. Visual Identity
**Glassmorphic (AetherFlow)**
The UI should utilize translucent panels, sophisticated background blurring, and subtle neon or jewel-toned accents to create a premium, modern "AetherFlow" aesthetic. Dark mode is the default, providing a striking contrast for colorful culinary imagery and emojis.

## 3. UX Philosophy
**Guided & Structured**
While the app uses a conversational interface, the user experience should feel structured and carefully guided. The AI should proactively steer the user toward actionable outcomes (e.g., generating a recipe, saving to the vault, tracking macros) rather than leaving them in an open-ended loop.
