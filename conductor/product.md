# Initial Concept
Palate is an AI-powered culinary application acting as a digital sous-chef. Built with Next.js, it provides a conversational interface to generate recipes, manage a local recipe vault, and explore culinary ideas.

# Product Vision

## Target Audience
**Elevated Home Chefs**
Designed for passionate home cooks aiming for MasterChef-level quality. The user appreciates high-quality ingredients, elegant presentation, and culinary creativity, seeking an assistant that matches their ambition.

## Core Capabilities
- **AI Sous-Chef (Sage):** A conversational intelligence engine that transforms pantry chaos or fleeting desires into culinary masterpieces.
- **Vault Management:** A robust, local-first Markdown vault for saving, organizing, and retrieving favorite recipes and nutritional data.

## Primary Focus
- **Pantry Management & Zero-Waste:** Intelligently utilizing available ingredients to reduce waste without compromising quality.
- **Culinary Exploration:** Curating elegant, high-quality dining experiences and discovering new flavor profiles.

## The 'Sage' Persona
- **Professional & Elegant:** Sage speaks concisely and with refined elegance. It exhibits deep mastery of the culinary arts and food science, avoiding robotic filler or excessive enthusiasm in favor of a highly capable, premium experience.
